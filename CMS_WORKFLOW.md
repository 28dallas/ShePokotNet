# CMS Workflow (Non-Technical Staff)

This workflow is for team members who do not code.

## 1. Open the CMS

Your organization admin will share the Sanity Studio URL after deployment.

## 2. Log in

Use your invited email account. Roles should be:
- `Editor` for creating and updating content
- `Administrator` (or publisher role) for final approval/publishing

## 3. Create or edit an Impact Story

1. Open **Impact Story** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Create new** (or open an existing story).
3. Fill required fields:
   - Title
   - Person / Group
   - Category
   - Published At
   - Cover Image
   - Image Alt Text
   - Summary
   - Observed Outcome
4. Set **Publishing Status**:
   - `draft` while editing
   - `published` when ready for website
5. Click **Publish**.

## 4. When changes appear on website

- Published content is fetched by the live website automatically.
- If not visible immediately, refresh after a short wait.

## 5. Basic editorial checklist

- Use clear, respectful language.
- Avoid unverified numbers.
- Add accurate image alt text.
- Keep dates correct.
- Use `draft` until reviewed.
